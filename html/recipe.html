Featured Recipes

The Espresso Martini

Vibe: Sophisticated, energetic, modern nightlife.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • .5 oz Coffee Liqueur (like Kahlúa)
  • 1 oz Freshly brewed espresso
  • .5 oz Simple syrup

Instructions: Shake vigorously with ice until frothy, strain into a chilled martini glass, and garnish with exactly three espresso beans.

The French 75

Vibe: Crisp, celebratory, upscale events and weddings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Gin
  • .5 oz Simple syrup
  • 3 oz Champagne (or Prosecco)
  • 3x Fresh Lemon Wedge

Instructions: Shake the gin, fresh lemon wedge, and syrup with ice. Strain into a champagne flute and top with the bubbly. Garnish with a Cocktail Cherry.

The Old Fashioned

Vibe: Timeless, moody, and spirit-forward.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Rye Whiskey or Bourbon
  • .5 oz Turbinado syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 2 dashes Orange Bitters

Instructions: Stir ingredients with a large ice cube in an Old Fashion Tin for 30sec. Strain over a fresh large cube in a rocks glass. Garnish with a flamed orange peel and Cocktail Cherry.

The Aperol Spritz

Vibe: Light, bubbly, and the ultimate patio drink.

Ingredients:

  • 3 oz Prosecco
  • 2 oz Aperol
  • 1 oz Club Soda

Instructions: Build over ice in a large wine glass. Add the Aperol, top with Prosecco and soda, and stir gently. Garnish with a large orange slice.

The Classic Daiquiri

Vibe: Simple, crisp, and a bartender's handshake.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Light Rum
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz Strawberry Syrup
  • .75 oz Simple Syrup

Instructions: Blend all ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.

The Dry Martini

Vibe: Sharp, strong, and highly personalized.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz London Dry Gin or Vodka
  • .5 oz Dry Vermouth
  • .5 oz Olive Juice

Instructions: Stir with ice in a mixing glass until freezing cold. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with an olive.

The Paloma

Vibe: Zesty, thirst-quenching, and Mexico's favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Blanco Tequila
  • .5 oz Fresh lime juice
  • Top with Grapefruit Soda (Squirt or Jarritos)

Instructions: Build in a highball glass over ice. Add tequila and lime juice, top with grapefruit soda. Stir gently and garnish with a grapefruit slice.

The Piña Colada

Vibe: Rich, creamy, and undeniably tropical.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Light Rum
  • 1.5 oz Cream of Coconut
  • 1 oz Pineapple juice
  • .5 oz Fresh lime juice

Instructions: Blend with a scoop of crushed ice. Pour into a hurricane glass and garnish with a pineapple wedge and a cherry.

The Classic Margarita

Vibe: Bright, refreshing, and universally loved.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Blanco Tequila
  • .75 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz Orange Liqueur (like Cointreau)
  • .5 oz Simple syrup

Instructions: Shake with ice, strain over fresh ice in a rocks glass with a salted rim. Garnish with a lime wheel.

The Mojito

Vibe: Tropical, minty, perfect for summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • .75 oz Simple syrup
  • 6-8 Fresh mint leaves
  • Club Soda

Instructions: Gently muddle mint and syrup in a shaker. Add rum, lime, and ice. Shake lightly, pour everything into a highball glass, and top with soda.

The Negroni

Vibe: Bitter, botanical, and an Italian classic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz London Dry Gin
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth

Instructions: Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass until very cold. Strain over one large ice cube in a rocks glass. Garnish with an orange peel.

The Whiskey Sour

Vibe: Silky, balanced, and a true test of technique.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • .75 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • .5 oz Simple syrup
  • 1 egg white or aquafaba

Instructions: "Dry shake" (no ice) all ingredients to emulsify the egg, then add ice and shake again until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled coupe glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a cocktail cherry.

The Manhattan

Vibe: Elegant, rich, historically significant.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Rye whiskey
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Instructions: Stir with ice in a mixing glass until well chilled. Strain into a chilled martini or coupe glass. Garnish with a Brandied Cherry.

The Moscow Mule

Vibe: Spicy, bubbly, and an iconic presentation.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz Vodka
  • .5 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz Ginger beer

Instructions: Build directly in a copper mug filled with ice. Squeeze in the lime juice, add the vodka, and top with ginger beer. Gently stir and garnish with a lime wedge.

The Cosmopolitan

Vibe: Tart, fruity, and an iconic cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz Citrus Vodka
  • .75 oz Orange Liqueur (like Cointreau)
  • .75 oz Fresh lime juice
  • .5 oz Cranberry juice

Instructions: Shake all ingredients with ice until very cold. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lime wheel or orange twist.
