The Espresso Martini

Vibe: Sophisticated, energetic, modern nightlife.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • .5 oz Coffee Liqueur (like Kahlúa)
  • 1 oz Freshly brewed espresso
  • .5 oz Simple syrup

Instructions: Shake vigorously with ice until frothy, strain into a chilled martini glass, and garnish with exactly three espresso beans.

The French 75

Vibe: Crisp, celebratory, upscale events and weddings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Gin
  • .5 oz Simple syrup
  • 3 oz Champagne (or Prosecco)
  • 3x Fresh Lemon Wedge

Instructions: Shake the gin, fresh lemon wedge, and syrup with ice. Strain into a champagne flute and top with the bubbly. Garnish with a Cocktail Cherry.

The Smoked Old Fashioned

Vibe: Timeless, moody, and spirit-forward.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Rye Whiskey or Bourbon
  • .5 oz Turbinado syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 2 dashes Orange Bitters

Instructions: Stir ingredients with a large ice cube in an Old Fashion Tin for 30sec. Strain over a fresh large cube in a rocks glass. Garnish with a flamed orange peel and Cocktail Cherry.

The Aperol Spritz

Vibe: Light, bubbly, and the ultimate patio drink.

Ingredients:

3 oz Prosecco

2 oz Aperol

1 oz Club Soda

Instructions: Build over ice in a large wine glass. Add the Aperol, top with Prosecco and soda, and stir gently. Garnish with a large orange slice.

The Classic Daiquiri

Vibe: Simple, crisp, and a bartender's handshake.

Ingredients:

2 oz Light Rum

1 oz Fresh lime juice

1 oz Strawberry Syrup

.75 oz Simple syrup

Instructions: Blend all ingredients with ice. Strain into a chilled coupe glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.

The Dry Martini

Vibe: Sharp, strong, and highly personalized.

Ingredients:

2 oz London Dry Gin or Vodka

.5 oz Dry Vermouth

.5 oz Olive Juice

Instructions: Stir with ice in a mixing glass until freezing cold. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with an olive.

The Paloma

Vibe: Zesty, thirst-quenching, and Mexico's favorite.

Ingredients:

2 oz Blanco Tequila

.5 oz Fresh lime juice

Top with Grapefruit Soda (Squirt or Jarritos)

Instructions: Build in a highball glass over ice. Add tequila and lime juice, top with grapefruit soda. Stir gently and garnish with a grapefruit slice.

The Piña Colada

Vibe: Rich, creamy, and undeniably tropical.

Ingredients:

2 oz Light Rum

1.5 oz Cream of Coconut

1 oz Pineapple juice

.5 oz Fresh lime juice

Instructions: Blend with a scoop of crushed ice. Pour into a hurricane glass and garnish with a pineapple wedge and a cherry.
